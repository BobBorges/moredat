{% translate 'Wordlist Translation' %}

{% translate 'Rationale' %}

{% translate 'When we know almost nothing about a language, it can be useful to start by translating lists of basic vocabulary and sets of short phrases (grammar paradigms). This app allows for the creation of word (or phrase) list-based Tasks.' %}

{% translate 'How it works' %}

{% translate 'Written stimuli are compiled into lists, and lists form the basis for tasks that can be assigned to research participants. In a task, a participant is presented with items in a list, one at a time, and they provide a response — either a recorded response (audio and optionally a written version and/or commentary), or an "I don't know" or "I don't remember". Responses are recorded as database entries and audio is saved to the media/recorded-audio directory.' %}
